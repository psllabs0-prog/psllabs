## Headline
Built because the shelf wasn't good enough.

## Body

I didn't set out to start a supplement company. I set out to find a daily longevity stack I could actually trust—and couldn't.

Everything on the shelf had the same problems. Proprietary blends that hid the doses. Hero ingredients at a tenth of what the studies used. "Clinically inspired" formulas with no citations, no COAs, and no way to verify what was in the capsule. Marketing that sounded like science and read like a billboard.

I spent years reading papers, comparing labels, and building my own protocol from single-ingredient bottles. It worked, but it was expensive, tedious, and impossible to recommend to anyone who didn't have forty hours to spare on PubMed.

PSL Labs is what I wished existed: a small, honest stack of three products—Foundation, Cellular Energy, and Recovery—each with every dose disclosed, each ingredient chosen for a specific mechanism, each batch tested by a lab we don't own and published openly.

We are not a wellness brand. We don't do before-and-after photos, miracle claims, or influencer formulas. We make reference-grade longevity compounds for people who read labels, check COAs, and care about what compounds actually do—not what an ad says they do.

The protocol is simple on purpose. Three compounds. One daily rhythm. Designed to compound over years, not weeks.

If that sounds like you, welcome. If you're looking for hype, you'll be disappointed—and that's intentional.
