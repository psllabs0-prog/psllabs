# PSL Labs Claims Framework

## Safe (structure/function with disclaimer)
- "Supports cellular health"
- "Supports healthy aging"
- "Supports NAD+ levels"
- "Supports mitochondrial function"
- "Supports the body's natural cellular processes"
- "Supports cellular energy production"
- "Helps maintain healthy [marker] already within normal range"

## Required disclaimer (every page with structure/function claims)
"These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration.
This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."

## Unsafe — never use
- "Treats / cures / prevents / heals [anything]"
- "Anti-aging" as a verb claim ("anti-aging supplement")
- "Boosts" or "increases" specific biomarkers without substantiation
- "Clinically proven" unless the product itself has clinical studies
- "Doctor recommended" without a signed endorsement and substantiation file
- Weight loss claims of any kind
- "Replaces" or "alternative to" any prescription medication
- "Natural Ozempic," "natural GLP-1," any comparison to drugs
- Before/after photography
- Specific outcome claims ("you will...")
