# PSL Labs Brand Reference

## Brand
PSL Labs is a premium longevity supplement brand. Three SKUs: Foundation
(daily longevity capsule), Cellular Energy (NAD+ pathway), Recovery
(mitochondrial function). Audience: biohackers, longevity-focused consumers,
the looksmaxxing/aesthetics overlap.

## Positioning
The daily protocol for the next thirty years. Clinical-grade longevity
compounds. Third-party verified every batch. Designed to compound.

## Voice
- Precise, not hyped. "5g of micronized creatine" not "ULTRA-PURE."
- Confident, not defensive. State what it does. Don't apologize.
- Educational, not pedagogical. Treat reader as smart friend.
- Reference-grade. Cite studies. Show COAs. Show dosing logic.
- Warm at the edges. Founder voice in FAQs and About; clinical in product copy.

## Always
- Disclose every dose. No proprietary blends.
- Cite studies on product pages.
- Use monospace for numbers, doses, batch IDs.
- Use serif for headlines.
- Use sans for body.
- Include FDA disclaimer on every page with structure/function claims.

## Never
- Promise weight loss, muscle growth, or disease prevention.
- Use words like "treats," "cures," "prevents," "boosts testosterone."
- Use before/after photos.
- Use neon, gradients, bevels, gym-bro imagery, DNA helixes, hexagons.
- Sound apologetic. Sound hype. Sound like a generic wellness brand.

## Reference brands (aesthetic and tone)
Function Health, Ritual, Momentous, Aesop, Le Labo, Apple product pages,
Linear, Arc Browser, Lifeforce.
